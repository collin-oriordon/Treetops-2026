Rules & Notes

  • -- Fourball (best ball): every player plays their own ball -- the lowest net score among the two partners counts as the team score for that hole.
  • -- Singles: standard match play, one-on-one, full course. Pairings announced Saturday morning.
  • -- Handicaps applied per USGA match play guidelines. Stroke index from each course's official card.
  • -- Conceded putts (gimmes) are at the opponent's discretion. Pick it up = it's good. No taking it back.
  • -- No out of bounds: everything will be played as a red lateral hazard.
  • -- In the event of a tie in total points, captains will decide on a tiebreaker.
